Biography

I am a fourth year Phd Candidate of computer science at Michigan State University, SEIT(SEcure and Intelligent Things) lab and eLANS(experimental Laboratory for Advanced Networks and Systems) lab. My research interests include signal processing, speech recognition, machine learning, AI-enabled mobile systems. I am co-advised by Dr. Xiao Li and Dr. Qiben Yan.
